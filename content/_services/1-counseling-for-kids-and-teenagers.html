We offer counseling in the entire spectrum of emotional, personal and psychosomatic problems (physical illnesses from headaches, stomach aches, allergies, dermal problems, etc that appear after a serious, stressful or difficult situation), divorce and post-divorce states, chronic diseases and pain, and more.

We advise you how to resolve problems related to the difficulties of defiant children and how to handle the situation when you get angry.

We review the maturity of your child and his readiness for school. If your child is not ready, we offer a 9 month INPP school program of exercises that help mature the central nervous system and ready your preschooler for school, all in a playful and fun way.

Is your child hyperactive, does he have problems with attention, learning or behavior? We will gladly advise you on this.

If you need more information about child development, personal grow, and healthy lifestyles for your child or teenager, do not hesitate to ask us.
